What is CRA Tax Settlement & Relief?

The CRA offers relief programs to help Canadians reduce or eliminate tax debts, penalties, and interest. At Canprosys, we negotiate directly with the CRA to secure the best outcome for your financial situation.

  • Reduce or eliminate penalties & interest
  • Negotiate affordable payment plans
  • Resolve years of unfiled taxes
  • Protect your assets from CRA collections

Types of CRA Relief Programs

Penalty & Interest Relief

Request relief from late-filing penalties, interest on overdue taxes, and other charges if you meet CRA’s taxpayer relief provisions.

Payment Arrangements

Negotiate structured monthly payments with CRA, preventing wage garnishments and bank account freezes.

Voluntary Disclosures Program (VDP)

File unreported income or past due taxes voluntarily to avoid prosecution and reduce penalties.

Taxpayer Relief Provisions

Get relief due to financial hardship, natural disasters, or CRA delays that made compliance difficult.

Who Qualifies for CRA Tax Relief?

Those facing financial hardship or serious illness

Small business owners with unpaid GST/HST or payroll taxes

Individuals penalized unfairly by CRA errors or delays

Canadians with unfiled tax returns or audit issues
