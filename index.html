Our Process

Our process is designed to make tax resolution simple, transparent, and stress-free. We take things one step at a time to figure out what you need, make a plan especially for you, and deal with the CRA's problems. We maintain clarity and confidence throughout the resolution process to the last consultation.

Consultation

We begin with a one-on-one meeting to discuss your financial issues and to map out the most appropriate direction to take with your tax situation.

Document Collection

Our experts help us assemble the necessary financial documentation, reports, and CRA correspondence to create a strong case.

Analysis & Strategy

We go through your documents and provide a tailor-made action plan to solve your tax problems in the most efficient way possible.

Implementation & Resolution

Our team represents you with the CRA and works toward achieving a fair settlement for your situation.
