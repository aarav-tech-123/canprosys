What Does the Tax Settlement Program Involve?

Payment Arrangements

In case you are unable to pay all your tax debt in one shot, you can negotiate and pay in instalments to CRA. This will enable you to pay taxes in small instalments.

Taxpayer Relief Provisions

The CRA’s Taxpayer Relief Provisions allow taxpayers to request the waiver of penalties and interest in exceptional circumstances, including illness, natural disasters, personal hardship, or CRA errors. Applications are submitted via Form RC4288.

Interest & Penalties Waiver

Taxpayers can get rid of or lower extra fees on overdue taxes if they can show reasonable cause, financial hardship, or errors beyond their control. This makes it easier for them to pay back their taxes and easing compliance burdens.
