Our Story

Canprosys Consultants Inc. was founded in YYYY with a simple mission: to help Canadians navigate the complex world of taxation and achieve financial peace of mind. What started as a small tax consultancy has grown into a comprehensive financial services firm serving clients across Canada.

Our founder, Michael Richardson, recognized that many Canadians were struggling with tax debt and complex CRA issues without access to expert guidance. He assembled a team of tax professionals, accountants, and former CRA advisors to provide specialized support to individuals and businesses.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of clients resolve over $28 million in tax debt and achieve financial stability. Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client success remains at the core of everything we do.
